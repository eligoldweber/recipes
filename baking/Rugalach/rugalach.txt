8 oz. cream cheese
2 sticks butter
(these should both be at room temperature)

combine 2 cups flour with 2 tablespoon sugar

cut butter into flour (use 2 knives).  add cream cheese.  mix well to form dough.
form into ball.  wrap in wax paper and refrigerate 24 hours.

divide dough into 4 pieces.  roll out each piece into thin circle.  cut into 16 triangles.  spread with raspberry jam, then sprinkle with cinnamon sugar, nuts and mini choc. chips.  roll each piece from fat end to point.  place on parchment covered cookie sheet.  bake 25-30 minutes at 350 degrees.  when cool, roll in powdered sugar.
